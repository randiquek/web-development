Creamy Chicken and Pasta Recipe

This creamy chicken recipe was one I came up with in my random creations of experimenting. This is a dish that uses chicken and heavy cream to give the sauce a good thickness similar to alfredo sauce. The tomato juice and diced tomatoes give it a nice light red color. I make this a lot (when I'm not too lazy). This dish makes about 2-4 servings but mostly 2 because you'll definitely come back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Maybe 3 cups of heavy cream
  • Teaspoon of flour
  • Package of chicken breast or thigh
  • Creole seasoning
  • Oregano
  • Thyme
  • Garlic/garlic powder
  • Shredded Mozerella
  • Peppers and onions
  • Spinach (if you want)
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tomato juice
  • Macaroni (whatever you prefer)
  • 2tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with creole seasoning and fry till it's cooked
  2. Set chicken aside and boil macaroni till done. Also set aside.
  3. Prep for sauce. In a large pan, cook the peppers and onions. Add in butter and mix flour until you have a nice roux mixture
  4. Slowly mix in heavy cream
  5. Add tomato juice and diced tomatoes
  6. Add in spinach, cheese, and other seasonings
  7. Add in pieces of chicken and coat it so it soaks in the sauce
  8. Serve over pasta
  9. Yum

Note: This is not an accurate representation of my recipe, as I measure and add things by eye. Feel free to ask if further clarification is needed
