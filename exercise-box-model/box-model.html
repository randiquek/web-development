Did you know that it snows metal on Venus?

The planet's surface is so hot that pyrite minerals vaporize into a metallic mist. At higher altitudes, the mist condenses into frost.

Other Weird Facts

  • Venus rotates clockwise on its axis, which is opposite to most planets. This is called retrograde rotation.
  • The planet rotates slowly, while it's atmosphere spins faster, which is called superrotation
  • There is the possibility of life not on the surface, but underground on the planet
